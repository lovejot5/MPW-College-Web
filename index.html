Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda

Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda was established in the year 1985 with the aim of imparting quality technical education in the field of Engineering and Technology. The institution caters to students of southern Punjab and is located on Bibiwala Road, approximately 3 km from Bathinda Bus Stand and 4.2 km from Bathinda Railway Junction.

The college is spread over a lush green campus of 22 acres and is committed to academic excellence, practical-based learning, and holistic student development.

Principal’s Message

Dr. Anuja Pupneja Principal, GPC Bathinda

Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda strives to impart practical and quality education along with sufficient job-oriented skills. The institution has been honored with multiple awards by the Government of Punjab for excellence in technical education.

We strongly emphasize the overall personality development of every student by encouraging participation in academics, sports, and co-curricular activities, enabling them to become competent professionals and responsible citizens.
